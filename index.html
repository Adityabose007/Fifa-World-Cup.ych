MANAGERS

LIONEL SCALONI

Yes, Lionel Scaloni successfully led Argentina to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup held in Qatar. Under his guidance, the Argentine national team secured their third World Cup title, marking their first triumph since 1986.

DIDIER DESCHAMPS

The manager of the French national team during the 2022 FIFA World Cup was Didier Deschamps. He led Les Bleus in their quest to defend the title they had won four years earlier. Despite a valiant effort, France finished as runners-up, losing to Argentina in an epic final that ended in a 3-3 draw and was ultimately decided by penalties.

ZLAKTO DALIC

Croatia’s manager for the 2022 World Cup is Zlatko Dalic. He successfully guided Croatia to the final of the 2018 World Cup, where they finished as runners-up after an intense match against France. Under Dalic’s leadership, Croatia also secured a place in the 2020 UEFA European Championship, although they were eliminated by Spain in the round of 16.

HAJIME MORIYASU

Japan’s national football team was led by Hajime Moriyasu during the 2022 FIFA World Cup held in Qatar. In a historic victory, Japan defeated Germany 2-1, causing quite a stir in the football world. Two Japan players, who also play their club football in Germany, scored the crucial goals that secured this remarkable win.
